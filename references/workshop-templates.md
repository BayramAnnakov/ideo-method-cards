## 5. Research Insight Report

A synthesis document that turns raw research findings into actionable insights. This is the primary deliverable for stakeholders and decision-makers.

### Template

```markdown
# Research Insight Report: {{PROJECT_NAME}}

**Date:** {{DATE}}
**Research Lead:** {{LEAD_NAME}}
**Methods Used:** {{METHOD_1}}, {{METHOD_2}}, {{METHOD_3}}
**Participants:** {{PARTICIPANT_COUNT}} ({{PARTICIPANT_DESCRIPTION}})
**Research Period:** {{START_DATE}} — {{END_DATE}}

---

## Executive Summary

{{3-5 sentence summary of the most important findings. Lead with the single most actionable insight. Written for someone who will only read this section.}}

## Key Insights

### Insight 1: {{INSIGHT_TITLE}}
**Confidence:** High / Medium / Low
**Evidence:** Observed in {{X}} of {{Y}} participants

{{2-3 sentence description of the insight.}}

> "{{SUPPORTING_QUOTE}}" — {{PARTICIPANT_ID}}

**Implication:** {{What this means for the product/business.}}

### Insight 2: {{INSIGHT_TITLE}}
{{Same structure as above. Include 3-7 insights total.}}

## Opportunity Areas

Based on the insights above, we identified these opportunity areas:

| # | Opportunity | Linked Insights | Potential Impact | Effort |
|---|------------|----------------|-----------------|--------|
| 1 | {{OPPORTUNITY}} | Insights {{X, Y}} | High / Medium / Low | High / Medium / Low |

## Recommendations

### Immediate (This Sprint)
1. {{ACTION}} — because {{INSIGHT_REFERENCE}}

### Short-Term (This Quarter)
1. {{ACTION}} — because {{INSIGHT_REFERENCE}}

### Needs More Research
1. {{QUESTION}} — suggested method: {{METHOD}}

## Appendix

### A. Participant Summary
| ID | Segment | Key Characteristics |
|----|---------|-------------------|
| P1 | {{SEGMENT}} | {{CHARACTERISTICS}} |

### B. Method Details
{{Brief description of how each method was conducted, any deviations from plan.}}

### C. Raw Data References
{{Links or references to interview recordings, photos, notes, affinity diagrams, etc.}}
```

### Usage Notes
- Lead with insights, not methods — stakeholders care about what you learned, not how
- Confidence levels help decision-makers calibrate how much to rely on each insight
- The Opportunity/Effort matrix helps prioritize without a separate meeting
- Keep the main report to 2-3 pages; use the appendix for supporting detail

---

## Template Usage Notes

- **Workshop Plan** -- Use when planning any single workshop session. Scale the agenda rows to match duration.
- **Method Recommendation Report** -- Use when the skill recommends methods for a challenge. Typically includes 2-5 methods.
- **Facilitation Guide Printout** -- Generate one per method. Designed to be printed or viewed on a tablet during facilitation.
- **Design Sprint Blueprint** -- Use for multi-day sprint planning. Adjust the number of days (default 5) to match the sprint format.
- **Research Insight Report** -- Use after completing research methods to synthesize findings into actionable insights for stakeholders.
